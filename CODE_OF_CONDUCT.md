# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project exists to produce a precise, trustworthy specification. We expect everyone taking part to act professionally and in good faith. Disagreement about technical substance is welcome and expected. Personal attacks, harassment, and bad-faith conduct are not.

Behaviour we expect:

- Engage with the technical argument, not the person making it.
- Assume good faith, and give precise, actionable feedback.
- Respect that the maintainers carry final responsibility for the normative text.
- Keep private what is shared with you in confidence, including a security report under embargo.

Behaviour that is not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or demeaning comments of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information without their consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or review.

## Scope

This applies in every project space, including issues, pull requests, commit messages, and any channel that represents the project, and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to **conduct@provii.app**. Reports are handled discreetly. Maintainers may remove or reject contributions and may restrict participation for conduct that breaches this policy. The maintainers' decision is final.
